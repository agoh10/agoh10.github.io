Why?

Our Team

Ashley Goh is a current junior at Nashua High School North. She has a passion for computer science and first learned Javascript in sixth grade. From there, she has learned Python 2, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, and Java. Using her experience, she developed this website in addition to drawing the images. By starting Fem in STEM, Ashley hopes to give other females the opportunity to have an early start in STEM as she did, and find their passion.

Ashley has worked as a soccer referee for three years. She also has experience in software, mechanical, and electrical engineering as a team mentor in BAE Systems' FOCUS Program. She has also tutored students in computer science through Digitize Hackathon's Mentorship Program.

Anjali is currently a junior at Green Level High School. She is passionate about both math and science and hopes more women can also find an interest in it, too. Anjali hopes that young girls will get exposed to a wide variety of STEM careers through Fem in STEM.

In school, Anjali enjoys participating in HOSA, Quiz Bowl, Photography Club, and Key Club. Anjali plays the piano, which she has been doing for almost 8 years. She also has 3 years of experience as a math tutor for children aged 5 to 14. For fun, she enjoys going on walks and watching Netflix.

She is passionate about making videos and uploads her work to her Youtube channel. Workshops

Workshops are short courses that cover a specific topic. Unlike our one-on-one tutoring option, workshops are taught in groups. Workshops should last around an hour, give or take 15 minutes. All workshops and services provided by Fem in STEM are voluntary and free. Feel free to email us at feminstemprogram@gmail.com if you have any questions or feedback!
